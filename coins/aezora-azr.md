## Coin Specs

• **PoW Algorithm:** Scrypt\
• **Premine:** 250,000 AZR \
• **PoW Blocks:** 1 - 250\
• **PoS Blocks:** Starting from 251\
• **Block Time**: 60 Seconds\
• **Maturity:** 100 Confirmations\
• **Prefix:** AZR addresses start with the capital letter "A"\
• **Ports:** 14725 (p2p) / 14724 (rpc)
